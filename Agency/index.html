Summit 1.0

Technical conference.

Summit 1.0 is a technical conference for UG, PG, research scholars, faculties, industrailists and startups. Professionals from reputed colleges, startups and industries will guide the participants in 4 major field of electrical and electronics. A complete exposure about the fields, the researches going on in those particular field and industrial perspective will be provided.

The 4 major fields are as follows:

  • Signals and communication
  • Robotics and automation
  • Power systems and energy
